About Us

Pioneering a Sustainable Future

At Solace Energy, we're more than just an electrician in Brisbane; we're your partner in navigating the path towards a more sustainable and efficient lifestyle. We've established ourselves as leaders in the field of electrical, solar, battery storage, air conditioning, and home automation solutions. Our mission is to empower homes and businesses alike with smart, sustainable energy solutions that not only meet today's needs but also pave the way for a greener tomorrow.

Our Journey

Founded on the principles of innovation, quality, and sustainability, Solace Energy has grown from a small, passionate team to a premier name in the industry. Our journey has been driven by a commitment to excellence and a deep understanding of the evolving energy landscape. By staying at the forefront of technology and environmental trends, we've been able to offer cutting-edge solutions that are both effective and eco-friendly.

Our Services

  • Electrical
    From minor repairs to major installations and switchboard upgrades, our expert electricians ensure your electrical systems are safe, reliable, and up to code.

  • Solar
    We specialise in high-quality solar panel installations and solar maintenance helping you harness the sun's energy and reduce your carbon footprint.

  • Battery Storage
    With our advanced battery storage solutions, you can store excess solar energy for use during peak hours or power outages, enhancing your energy independence.

  • Air Conditioning
    Our efficient ducted and split air conditioning systems offer the perfect balance of comfort and energy savings, tailored to suit your specific needs. We also offer maintenance services.

  • Smart Home Integration
    Step into the future with our home automation services, offering unparalleled convenience and control over your living environment.
